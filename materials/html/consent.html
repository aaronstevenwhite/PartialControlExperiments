Project Title

Acquiring the semantics and pragmatics of attitude verbs

Purpose of the Study

This research is being conducted by Dr. Valentine Hacquard and Dr. Jeffrey Lidz at the University of Maryland, College Park. We are inviting you to participate in this research project because you are an adult native speaker with no language impairments. The purpose of this research project is to better understand how humans understand language.

Procedures

The procedures involve the following. The study will last up to 60 minutes, although most sessions are shorter. You will be asked to silently read sentences or listen to words over headphones. You might be asked to evaluate and answer questions by pushing response buttons on a computer, for example: “Is this a natural sentence of English?”

Potential Risks and Discomforts

 

 

There are no known risks associated with this procedure.

Potential Benefits

This research is not designed to benefit you personally, but the results may help the investigator learn more about the processing of human language. We hope that, in the future, other people might benefit from this study through improved understanding of how people understand language.

Confidentiality

We will make every effort to keep your personal information confidential. Any potential loss of confidentiality will be minimized by recording the data we collect with a numeric code in place of your name before further processing. If we write a report or article about this research project, your identity will be protected to the maximum extent possible; your name and/or initials will never be used, and any description of personal information will be limited to including your age in a description of average participant age and your gender in a description of the distribution of participant gender. Your information may be shared with representatives of the University of Maryland, College Park or governmental authorities if you or someone else is in danger or if we are required to do so by law.

Medical Treatment

The University of Maryland does not provide any medical, hospitalization or other insurance for participants in this research study, nor will the University of Maryland provide any medical treatment or compensation for any injury sustained as a result of participation in this research study, except as required by law.

Right to Withdraw and Questions
